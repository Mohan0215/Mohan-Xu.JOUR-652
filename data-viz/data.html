International Students Facing Stress at UMD

By Mohan Xu

Examing the international student enrollment between 2014 and 2018

Year should be 2014/2015(Fall/Spring); 2015/2016; 2016/2017;2018. The number of international students has been constantly high.

Source: Data collected by UMD

The Number of Stress

N represents the number of clinicians because they are asked to choose one primary concern (for example, the top-most concern) per client. Each year, stress was the top four conern among these concerns. However, the percentage of stress was different. It was 5.9%, 5.5%, 5.7% and 5.8% between 2015 and 2018.

Source: Data collected by the Center for Collegiate Mental Health at Penn State University
